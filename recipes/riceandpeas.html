Ingredients

  • Vegetable Oil
  • White Oinions
  • Celery
  • carrots
  • Garlic
  • Garlic
  • Dried Oregano
  • Water

  1. White Sauce Lasagna Base

    • Place everything in a sauce pot and mix well.
    • Apply medium heat, bring to a boil and simmer for 2-3 min.
    • Mix well from time to time.
    • Set aside to cool.
    • Store inside the chiller until use. Apply cold during the lasagna assembly.

  2. Meatless Spaghetti Sauce Base

    • Add oil to the pot and apply medium heat.
    • Saute onions, garlic, celery and carrots. Add dried oregano and continue to saute until fragrant.
    • Add the prepared The Vegetarian Butcher NoMince. Saute until well incorporated.
    • Season with Knorr Aromat Seasoning Powder then add Knorr Tomato Paste. Mix well.
    • Add water, tomato sauce and the rest of the ingredients. Mix well and bring to a boil.
    • Simmer for 5-10 min. in low heat.
    • Set aside to cool. Apply cold during the lasagna assembly.

  3. Loaded Meatless Lasagna Assembly

    • Brush the bottom and side of the lasagna pan with soft butter.
    • Add ample amount of white sauce lasagna base.
    • Add the pre-cooked lasagna sheets. Add ample amount of meatless spaghetti sauce and
      top it with ample amount of white sauce lasagna base, then top with grated quick melt cheese.
    • Do this for another 2 layers. Make sure to add enough sauce to make sure that its moist and flavorful.
    • For the topmost part, cover with a lasagna sheet. Add ample amount of white sauce
      lasagna base covering mostly the lasagna sheet.
    • Finish with ample amount of grated quick melted cheese. Give it a tap for everything to set and cover with foil.
    • Cook in a pre-heated convectional oven 170Â°C for 15 minutes. Afterwards, remove the foil and cook for
      another 15 minutes until it boils and the top has a nice gratinated color.
